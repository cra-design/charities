Request publicly available data from the List of charities (Charities Listings)

Use this form to request an electronic version of publicly available data from the List of charities. The data is provided in Comma Separated Values (CSV), and is sent by email or through a digital platform, depending on the size of the file. Please note that these requests are processed on a first-come, first-served basis. As a result, processing times can vary.

If you are a member of the media, contact a media relations representative. We are committed to processing your request as quickly as possible. Please note that most requests require at least six business days to complete.

Criteria

The following criteria will be applied to the list and the registered charity information return.


Designation code

The CRA gives all registered charities a designation based on structure, source of funding, and mode of operation.

Category code

The CRA assigns all registered charities a category code that reflects their purposes and activities.

Privacy notice

Personal information is collected under the authority of the Income Tax Act and is used to complete your request for publicly available charity information. Incomplete or inaccurate contact information may result in your request being abandoned.

Information you send to the Canada Revenue Agency (CRA) by email is not secure while it is in transit. Do not include confidential information such as your social insurance number or personal tax information on this form.

Information is described in personal information bank Charities, CRA PPU 200 in the CRA chapter of Info Source. Personal information is protected under the Privacy Act and individuals have a right of protection, access to and correction or notation of their personal information. Please be advised that you are entitled to complain to the Privacy Commissioner of Canada regarding our handling of your information.
