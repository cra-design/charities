List of universities outside Canada registered as qualified donees

Use the sortable tables below to confirm whether a university outside Canada is registered or revoked. 

Registered universities are qualified donees; therefore, they can issue official donation receipts and are eligible to receive gifts from registered charities.

Revoked universities are no longer qualified donees; therefore, they cannot issue official donation receipts and are not eligible to receive gifts from registered charities.
